sixtyfour

A 64×64 pixel display in your browser. Part clock, part canvas, part toy — sixtyfour is a creative showcase built around the constraints and charm of a tiny pixel grid.

The display

Every mode and animation is rendered on a grid of 4,096 virtual pixels. The grid fills the page. Everything else — menus, controls, settings — stays hidden until you need it, fading in when you move outside the grid and retreating again once you're done.

Modes

  • Animations — a built-in library of looping, generative pixel animations
  • Digital clock — the time, rendered in pixel digits
  • Analogue clock — a ticking clock face, pixel by pixel
  • Ripple pool — click or touch to disturb the surface
  • Games — classics and originals on a 64×64 canvas
  • Album art — pixel-rendered covers, with live Last.fm support coming

Built with

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step. Hosted on Cloudflare Pages.
